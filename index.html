About Me

If I went to Hogwarts, I would be a Ravenclaw.

In high school, I took Calculus, Physics, and Chemistry 12 for fun even when I was planning on going into the arts.

After graduating, I became an ESL teacher because I love language and helping others. But over the years, I realised I wasn't approaching teaching with the same enthusiasm as I did at first. I knew I would miss my students, but I wanted to be constantly challenged by my work.

In the tech industry, devs have to constantly upgrade themselves. When I first heard this, I knew it was a plus rather than a minus for me. So far, I've found that developing solutions and identifying bugs can be a mix of frustration and accomplishment, but nonetheless, I love the process. When the aha! moment strikes, it makes it all worth it.
